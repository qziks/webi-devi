Top Travel Cities

The travel iconic cities of Sri Lanka with many amazing activities which attract a million annually

Colombo City

Sri Lankan capital with National Museum, Gangaramaya Temple & ocean side promenade Galle Face Green.

Ella Town

Located in the vast tea plantations of Sri Lanka’s cultural center, Ella is one of the most popular places with adventure activities such as hike Little Adam’s Peak, flying Ravana zipline, taking the train to Haputale, etc

Galle City

Galle Fort, Galle Lighthouse, and the National Maritime Museum are key attractions in this Sri Lankan coastal city.

Kandy City

Large hilly Sri Lankan city containing the Temple of the Tooth shrine, which houses a Buddhist relic.

Mirissa Town

The stunning Sri Lankan beaches, excellent surfing waves, and waterways abounding with marine life ranging from turtles to large blue whales.

Nagombo City

Sri Lankan city is famous for its Dutch Fort, Hamilton Canal, and St. Mary's Church in Negombo Lagoon.
